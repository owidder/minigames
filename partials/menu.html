ngm1

Drag each colored circle with a number to the right or the left.

  • To left, if it is smaller than the number of the last circle with the same color
  • If the number is the same or you see the color the first time you can drag it to the left or the right
  • For each right decision you get the number added to your points ('P:') and the number of rounds ('R:') is increased by 1
  • For each wrong decision you get the number subtracted from your points and you loose one life
  • After 3 lost lifes you're dead
    • Thanks to angularjs.or and d3js.org
